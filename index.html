A new tech school Made in Luxembourg

The Luxembourg Tech School (LTS for short) is a new school concept. It is for 15-20 years old students who are passionate about the digital realm and eager to learn and apply technology with business.

Supported by Digital Luxembourg, the program will be hosted at the Athénée de Luxembourg. The pilot will launch in September with about 20 students. They will follow the program, get personalized coaching and work on their own projects that they will showcase at the end of each block.

How it works Meet a proven methodology

With over 10 years of teaching experience at the University, Asst-Prof. Dr. Sergio Coronado developed a method called Speed Project Management that focuses on creating and maintaining a continous dynamic environment.

Every year, he applied it directly in his classes with great success and overly positive feedback from the students. Here's how it works.

Open scope selection

Do I find it interesting? Do I care about it?

Nothing kills your motivation more than working on something that you do not feel passionate about. There is no secret. You heard the "do what you love". Well, once you do what you really like, you will discover the kind of motivation, drive and success that will propel you farer forward than any other method.

That is why we allow you to work on your own project. The scope is wide open. You select what you like. We help, guide and advise you at every moment.

"Flow" work assignements

Enjoy the work and commit fully.

You have your scope selected, you love it and are deeply motivated to make it happen. Now, your muscle needs to flex and results needs to be delivered. The "Flow" is the key to produce constant, sustainable and high quality results.

Each week you have assignements, specific to your project. Those are not tests nor exams, but are clear goals and challenges that you will need to work on. This will create a measurable and trackable progress that will excite you, week after week.

And keeping up the rhythm

Weekly follow-up and coaching.

Every week, we meet and follow up on the progress. This is the moment to reflect on the work done, the problems found and the next steps to come. The rhythm is not about rushing things and burning out, it is about building a speed at wich you are able to make serious progress day after day, week after week and month after month.

Though very personalised coaching, you will get all the required information that you need to move forward. Whether it is about ideas, algorithms, user experiences, designs, strategies, marketing, business, sales or anything else, we got your back!

An Epic Program Fun, Exciting & Challenging

Starting in September 2016, the program is organised into 3 blocks / courses. Each course is 12 week long and centered on one specific technology domain. At the end of each course, you (the students) will have the opportunity to present your project!

{ Game Dev }

  • How to develop
  • From idea to launch
  • Games, business, serious games
  • Sharpen your coding skills
  • Learn game development kits and tools
September to November 2016

{ Big Data }

  • Introduction to Machine Learning (ML)
  • Create Models and predict prices
  • Analyse online reviews
  • Code with Python and ML libraries
  • Learn all about Cloud Computing
January to March 2017

{ FinTech }

  • How financial transactions work
  • Keeping data secured
  • Hot topics (BitCoint, Crowdfunding, ...)
  • Deep dive into databases
  • Learn security and encryption
April to June 2017
